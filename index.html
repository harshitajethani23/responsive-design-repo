An ice cream cone is a cone-shaped, edible pastry, typically made of wafer or waffle, used to hold and serve ice cream, allowing for easy handheld consumption.

Softy

Softy ice cream, also known as soft serve, is a frozen dessert characterized by its soft, creamy, and light texture, achieved by incorporating air during the freezing process, resulting in a semi-frozen state.

Strawberry Icecream

Strawberry ice cream is a frozen dessert known for its pink or light red color and sweet, fruity flavor reminiscent of fresh strawberries, typically made with milk, cream, sugar, and strawberries.
